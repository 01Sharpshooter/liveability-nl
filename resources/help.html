About

The extension provides information about the quality of life in all areas of the Netherlands based on their 4-digit zipcode. This includes the most up-to-date situation and the improvements of the neighborhoods.

The information presented by this extension is based on the official open data of the Leefbaarometer, provided by the Ministry of the Interior and Kingdom Relations.

Last year of measurement: 2018
